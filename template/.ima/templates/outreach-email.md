# Outreach Templates

## Initial Outreach — Email

**Subject Line Options:**
- `[Brand] × [Influencer Name] — Love Your [Specific Content Reference]`
- `Collaboration Opportunity: [Brand] + [Influencer Name]`
- `We'd Love to Work With You, [Name]!`

---

**Template:**

```
Subject: [Subject Line]

Hi [Name],

[Specific compliment about a recent post/project — reference specific content that shows you've done research. 1-2 sentences.]

I'm [Your Name] from [Brand]. [One sentence about what the brand does, relevant to their niche.]

We've been following your content on [platform] and really admire how you [specific quality — e.g., "make sustainable fashion feel accessible and exciting"]. Your audience aligns perfectly with the community we're building.

We'd love to explore a collaboration:
• [Brief campaign description — what they'd create]
• Compensation: [rate or "happy to discuss what works for you"]
• Timeline: [approximate dates]
• Products: [what they'd receive]

Would you be open to a quick chat this week? I'm flexible on timing.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title] | [Brand]
[Email] | [Website]
```

## Follow-Up 1 (Day 3-4)

```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject]

Hi [Name],

Just following up on my message from [day]. I know you're probably busy creating amazing content!

Quick recap: [Brand] would love to partner with you on [brief description]. We think your [specific quality] would be a perfect fit.

Happy to jump on a quick 10-minute call or answer any questions over email — whatever works best for you.

[Your Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up 2 (Day 7-8)

```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject]
```

## Rate Negotiation — Response Templates

### When Rate Is Too High
```
Thanks for sharing your rates, [Name]! I appreciate the transparency.

Our budget for this campaign is in the [range] range for [content type]. 

Would you be open to exploring:
• A slightly adjusted scope (e.g., [fewer deliverables])?
• A base fee of $[amount] + performance bonus based on [metric]?
• A long-term partnership with a per-post rate?

We'd love to find something that works for both sides.
```

### When Rate Is Within Budget
```
That works well for us, [Name]! Let me put together the full campaign brief and send it over.

Quick confirms:
• Deliverables: [list]
• Timeline: [dates]
• Rate: $[amount] for [scope]
• Payment: [terms]

I'll also include our content guidelines and FTC disclosure requirements.
```

## Thank You — Post-Campaign

```
Hi [Name],

Just wanted to say a huge thank you for the incredible content you created for [Brand]! The [specific content piece] performed amazingly — [share a metric if appropriate].

Your audience clearly resonated with it, and we loved working with you.

We'd love to keep the relationship going. Would you be interested in:
• Becoming a brand ambassador?
• Collaborating on our next campaign in [month]?

Either way, thank you for bringing [Brand] to life so authentically.

Best,
[Your Name]
```
