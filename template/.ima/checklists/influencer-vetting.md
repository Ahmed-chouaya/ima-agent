# Influencer Vetting Checklist

Use this checklist when vetting any influencer for a potential partnership. Check off each item as you verify it.

---

## Basic Profile Verification
- [ ] Profile is public and accessible
- [ ] Bio is professional and niche-relevant
- [ ] Profile photo is professional/authentic
- [ ] Account age is reasonable (not brand new)
- [ ] Handle/username is professional
- [ ] Contact information available (email in bio or management)

## Content Quality
- [ ] Content is high-quality (visual, audio, editing)
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule (at least 2-3x/week)
- [ ] Content themes align with brand niche
- [ ] Content style matches brand aesthetic
- [ ] Mix of content formats (not just one type)
- [ ] Captions are well-written and engaging
- [ ] Hashtag strategy is relevant and not spammy

## Engagement Health
- [ ] Engagement rate is within tier benchmarks (see `knowledge-base/metrics/engagement-benchmarks.md`)
- [ ] Comments are genuine and relevant to content
- [ ] Creator responds to comments (community engagement)
- [ ] Comment-to-like ratio is healthy (1-5% of likes)
- [ ] Engagement is consistent across posts (not spiky)
- [ ] No signs of engagement pods

## Follower Authenticity
- [ ] Follower count matches tier expectations
- [ ] Following-to-follower ratio is reasonable
- [ ] No suspicious follower spikes (check growth history)
- [ ] Followers appear to be real accounts (sample check)
- [ ] Follower demographics align with target audience
- [ ] No evidence of purchased followers

## Brand Safety
- [ ] No controversial content in recent posts (last 6 months)
- [ ] No history of public controversies
- [ ] Content is appropriate for brand values
- [ ] No competitor partnerships that create conflicts
- [ ] No inappropriate language or imagery
- [ ] Political content is within acceptable range for brand

## Sponsored Content Evaluation
- [ ] Has experience with brand partnerships (if required)
- [ ] Sponsored content maintains quality of organic content
- [ ] Brand integrations feel natural and authentic
- [ ] Proper FTC disclosures on existing sponsored content
- [ ] Audience responds positively to sponsored content
- [ ] Not over-saturated with sponsorships (>50% sponsored = concern)

## Audience Alignment
- [ ] Audience demographics match target market
- [ ] Audience interests align with brand category
- [ ] Audience location matches campaign geography
- [ ] Audience engagement level is healthy

## Final Assessment
- [ ] Overall score calculated using IMA scoring framework
- [ ] Red flag check completed (see `knowledge-base/metrics/red-flags.md`)
- [ ] Rate assessment completed (see `knowledge-base/metrics/pricing-guides.md`)
- [ ] Recommendation documented (Proceed / Caution / Reject)
- [ ] Notes added for handoff to outreach team

---

**Result:** ✅ PROCEED / ⚠️ PROCEED WITH CAUTION / ❌ DO NOT PROCEED
