# Brand Safety Checklist

Use when evaluating an influencer for brand safety risks before partnership.

---

## Content Review (Last 6-12 Months)
- [ ] No hate speech, discrimination, or offensive content
- [ ] No excessive profanity inappropriate for brand
- [ ] No graphic or violent content
- [ ] No illegal activity depicted or promoted
- [ ] No drug/substance abuse content (unless relevant to niche)
- [ ] No sexually explicit content (unless brand category appropriate)
- [ ] No promotion of dangerous behaviors or misinformation
- [ ] Content aligns with brand values and image

## Political & Social Content
- [ ] Assessed political content sensitivity level
- [ ] No extreme political positions that conflict with brand
- [ ] Social justice positions (if any) align with brand stance
- [ ] No participation in harassment campaigns
- [ ] Cultural sensitivity is appropriate

## History & Reputation
- [ ] No past "canceled" events or major controversies
- [ ] No ongoing legal issues
- [ ] No public feuds with other creators or brands
- [ ] No negative press coverage
- [ ] No history of deleting controversial content
- [ ] Search results are clean (Google "[name] controversy")

## Competitor & Conflict Check
- [ ] No active partnerships with direct competitors
- [ ] No exclusivity agreements that conflict
- [ ] No negative mentions of your brand or products
- [ ] No partnerships with controversial brands
- [ ] Sponsorship diversity is healthy (not all one category)

## Audience Safety
- [ ] Audience demographics are appropriate for brand
- [ ] Comment sections are not toxic or hostile
- [ ] No presence of bots or fake engagement
- [ ] Community culture aligns with brand values

## Compliance Check
- [ ] Influencer properly discloses existing paid partnerships
- [ ] FTC/regulatory compliance history is clean
- [ ] No history of undisclosed sponsorships
- [ ] Understands and will follow disclosure requirements

---

## Risk Rating

| Risk Level | Criteria | Action |
|:----------:|----------|--------|
| 🟢 **Low** | All boxes checked, no concerns | Proceed |
| 🟡 **Medium** | 1-2 minor concerns | Proceed with monitoring plan |
| 🟠 **High** | Multiple concerns or 1 major | Add protective contract clauses |
| 🔴 **Critical** | Major safety risk identified | Do not proceed |
