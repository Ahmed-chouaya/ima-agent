## Pricing Factors

### What Drives Price UP
- High engagement rate (above tier average)
- Niche expertise (specialized creators charge more)
- Exclusivity requirements
- Usage rights (for ads, website, extended periods)
- Rush delivery
- Scripted/directed content (less creative freedom)
- Multiple revision rounds
- Celebrity or verified status

### What Drives Price DOWN
- Long-term partnerships (3+ months, discounted per-post rate)
- Multi-platform bundles
- Product-only deals (nano/micro may accept)
- Creative freedom (less production burden)
- Emerging/growing creators (investing in potential)
- Off-peak seasons

## Compensation Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Flat Fee** | Fixed payment per deliverable | Most common, straightforward |
| **Product Only** | Free products in exchange for content | Nano influencers, genuine fans |
| **Performance Based** | Payment tied to results (clicks, sales) | Conversion campaigns |
| **Hybrid** | Reduced cash + product + bonus | Budget flexibility |
| **Revenue Share** | % of sales (affiliate model) | Long-term partnerships |
| **Retainer** | Monthly fee for ongoing content | Ambassador programs |

## Negotiation Tips

1. **Always research first** — Know the market rate before negotiating
2. **Start with value** — Emphasize what you bring beyond money
3. **Bundle for discounts** — Multi-post or multi-platform packages
4. **Offer performance bonuses** — Align incentives with results
5. **Be transparent about budget** — Honesty builds trust
6. **Think long-term** — Lower rate for ongoing vs. one-off
7. **Include product value** — Products count toward compensation
8. **Consider usage rights separately** — Don't expect unlimited rights at base rate
