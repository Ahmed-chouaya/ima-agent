# Instagram Platform Guide

## Platform Overview
- **Users:** 2+ billion monthly active users
- **Demographics:** Primarily 18-44 age group; trending younger with Reels
- **Content Types:** Feed Posts, Reels, Stories, Carousels, Live, Guides
- **Best For:** Visual brands, lifestyle, fashion, beauty, food, fitness, travel

## Key Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- **Reels** are prioritized over static posts in discovery
- Watch time and re-watches are primary Reels signals
- Saves and shares are weighted heavily (more than likes)
- Carousel posts get high engagement due to multiple swipes
- Consistent posting schedule is rewarded
- Comments that generate replies signal quality content

## Influencer Discovery on Instagram

### Search Strategies
- **Hashtag search**: Browse niche hashtags, check "Top" and "Recent" tabs
- **Explore page**: Search keywords to find trending creators
- **Similar accounts**: Use "Suggested for you" on creator profiles
- **Tagged photos**: Check brand and competitor tagged photos
- **Collaborator posts**: Look for collab posts with similar brands
- **Reels search**: Search keywords within the Reels tab

### Key Hashtag Categories to Research
- Industry hashtags: `#sustainablefashion`, `#fitnessmotivation`
- Micro-community hashtags: `#ecofriendlylifestyle`, `#homeworkout`
- Branded competitor hashtags: Check what competitors promote
- Trending event hashtags: Seasonal or cultural events

## Best Posting Times (General)
- **Weekdays:** 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM (audience timezone)
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Stories:** Throughout the day (stays 24 hours)
- _Note: Always check specific audience insights for the influencer_

## Instagram-Specific Red Flags
- Following-to-follower ratio > 0.5 for accounts > 50K followers
- Engagement rate below 1% for accounts with 10K-100K followers
- Comment sections with only emoji responses
- Sudden follower jumps of 10K+ without viral content
- Stories with zero views despite large followings
- Very high Reels views but low profile engagement

## Disclosure Requirements
- Use `#ad` or `#sponsored` clearly visible (not buried in hashtags)
- Use Instagram's "Paid Partnership" tag when available
- Must be in the first line of caption or prominently placed
- Stories must have visible disclosure overlay
