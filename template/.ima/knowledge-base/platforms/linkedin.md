# LinkedIn Platform Guide

## Platform Overview
- **Users:** 1+ billion members, ~300M monthly active users
- **Demographics:** Professionals 25-55; decision-makers, B2B audience
- **Content Types:** Posts, articles, newsletters, video, documents, polls, Live
- **Best For:** B2B brands, SaaS, professional services, thought leadership, recruiting

## Key Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- **Dwell time** (time spent reading) is a key signal
- Posts with zero external links get better distribution
- Carousel/document posts get high engagement
- Comments from other creators boost visibility
- First 60-90 minutes of engagement determine reach
- Consistency matters (3-5 posts/week is ideal)
- LinkedIn prioritizes content from your network over external content

## Influencer Discovery on LinkedIn

### Key Differences
- LinkedIn "influencers" are thought leaders, not lifestyle creators
- Focus on expertise and authority rather than aesthetics
- B2B decision-makers are the primary audience

### Search Strategies
- Search industry keywords and check top posts
- Browse industry-specific hashtags
- Check LinkedIn's "Top Voices" and creator programs
- Look at speakers from industry conferences
- Review company page followers and commenters

## Disclosure Requirements
- `#ad` or `#sponsored` clearly visible in post
- LinkedIn has no built-in paid partnership tag (unlike Instagram)
- Written disclosure at beginning of post
