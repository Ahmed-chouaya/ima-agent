# YouTube Platform Guide

## Platform Overview
- **Users:** 2.5+ billion monthly active users
- **Demographics:** Broadest age range (18-65+); strong across all demographics
- **Content Types:** Long-form video, Shorts, Live, Community posts
- **Best For:** In-depth reviews, tutorials, thought leadership, B2B, evergreen content

## Key Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- **Watch time** is the primary ranking signal
- **Click-through rate (CTR)** from thumbnails drives impressions
- Audience retention curve (do people stay past the first 30 seconds?)
- Session time (does your video lead to more YouTube watching?)
- Shorts algorithm is separate from long-form (similar to TikTok FYP)
- Search-driven content has long-tail discovery (SEO matters)

## Influencer Discovery on YouTube

### Search Strategies
- **Keyword search**: Search niche terms, filter by recent uploads
- **Browse niche channels**: Check channel recommendations
- **YouTube search suggestions**: Auto-complete reveals popular searches
- **Social Blade**: Research channel growth stats and rankings
- **Competitor brand mentions**: Search "[brand] review" or "[brand] unboxing"
- **YouTube Creator partnerships page**: Some creators list contact info

### Key Metrics to Evaluate
- **Subscriber count** (but views matter more than subs)
- **Average views per video** (last 10-15 videos)
- **View-to-subscriber ratio** (healthy: 10-20%+ of subs per video)
- **Comment-to-view ratio** (healthy: 1-3% of views)
- **Upload consistency** (weekly or bi-weekly is ideal)

## Best Posting Times (General)
- **Weekdays:** 2-4 PM (for evening peak viewing)
- **Best days:** Thursday, Friday, Saturday
- **Upload time should be 2-3 hours before peak viewing**

## YouTube-Specific Red Flags
- Sub count much higher than average views (dead channel or bought subs)
- Disabled comments or hidden like/dislike counts
- Sudden subscriber jumps without corresponding view increases
- Shorts-only channels with no long-form (limited trust-building)

## Disclosure Requirements
- Verbal disclosure in the first 30 seconds of video
- "Includes paid promotion" checkbox in YouTube Studio
- Written disclosure in video description
- Overlay text for integrations/mentions
