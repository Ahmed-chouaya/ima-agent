# TikTok Platform Guide

## Platform Overview
- **Users:** 1.5+ billion monthly active users
- **Demographics:** Strongest with 16-34 age group; growing across all ages
- **Content Types:** Short videos (15s-10min), LIVE, Stories, Photo Mode
- **Best For:** Entertainment, trends, authentic content, Gen Z/Millennial reach

## Key Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- **Watch time** is the #1 signal (completion rate, re-watches)
- Content is distributed based on interest, not followers (FYP-driven)
- Using trending sounds/effects boosts distribution
- Early engagement (first 30-60 min) determines viral potential
- Consistent posting (1-3x/day) is rewarded
- Niche content often outperforms generic content
- Duets and stitches increase reach via existing audiences

## Influencer Discovery on TikTok

### Search Strategies
- **Keyword search**: Search niche terms in the search bar
- **Hashtag browsing**: Check niche and trending hashtags
- **TikTok Creator Marketplace**: Official platform for brand-creator matching
- **Trending sounds**: Find creators using popular niche-relevant sounds
- **Comment sections**: Top commenters on viral niche content often are creators
- **"Similar to" recommendations**: Follow the algorithm's suggestions

### Key Hashtag Categories
- Niche hashtags: `#BookTok`, `#FitTok`, `#CleanTok`, `#FoodTok`
- Trend hashtags: Currently trending challenges or themes
- Product hashtags: `#TikTokMadeMeBuyIt`, `#[product]review`

## Best Posting Times (General)
- **Weekdays:** 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-11 PM
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Thursday, Friday
- **Consistency matters more than timing** on TikTok
- _Note: TikTok's global algorithm means timezone is less critical_

## TikTok-Specific Red Flags
- Very high view counts but near-zero comments (possible view bots)
- Engagement that doesn't correlate with view counts
- All videos get exactly similar view counts (not natural)
- No organic trending content (only "viral" when sponsored)
- Comments disabled or heavily filtered

## Disclosure Requirements
- Use `#ad` or `#sponsored` in video text overlay and caption
- TikTok's "Branded Content" toggle must be enabled
- Disclosure must be visible without expanding caption
- Audio disclosure recommended for video content
