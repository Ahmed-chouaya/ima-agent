# X (Twitter) Platform Guide

## Platform Overview
- **Users:** 500+ million monthly active users
- **Demographics:** Skews male, 25-49; strong for news, tech, finance, politics
- **Content Types:** Tweets/posts, threads, Spaces (audio), video
- **Best For:** Thought leadership, B2B, tech, finance, news, community building

## Key Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- Engagement within first hour is critical
- Replies and quote tweets weighted higher than likes
- Threads perform well for long-form engagement
- Video content getting increased algorithmic boost
- Premium/verified accounts get distribution advantages
- Community Notes affect trust signals

## Content Formats for Influencer Marketing

| Format | Best For | Avg ER | Notes |
|--------|----------|:---:|-------|
| Tweet/Post | Quick awareness | 0.5-2% | Concise, high volume |
| Thread (5-10 tweets) | Education, storytelling | 1-3% | High engagement if valuable |
| Video Post | Product demo | 1-4% | Growing importance |
| Quote Tweet | Endorsement | 1-3% | Authentic engagement style |
| Spaces (Audio) | Thought leadership | N/A | Good for B2B and niche |

## Engagement Rate Benchmarks (X, 2025-2026)

| Tier | Posts | Threads | Video |
|------|:-----:|:-------:|:-----:|
| Nano (1K-10K) | 1-3% | 2-5% | 2-4% |
| Micro (10K-50K) | 0.5-2% | 1-3% | 1-3% |
| Mid (50K-500K) | 0.3-1% | 0.5-2% | 0.5-2% |
| Macro (500K+) | 0.1-0.5% | 0.3-1% | 0.3-1% |

_Note: X engagement rates declined ~48% YoY in 2025. Platform is best for niche B2B and thought leadership._

## Disclosure Requirements
- `#ad` or `#sponsored` in the tweet/post
- Clear disclosure at beginning, not buried in hashtags
- Thread campaigns need disclosure in the first tweet
